About us:

Movie Review is designed so that with one easy click of a button, you can pull up information on a movie that you would like to watch.

This website was created by a collaborative effort of a small band of coders to produce an application to make your life a little easier. It is our hope to help you save time and not have to watch a movie that just doesn't suit your needs.
